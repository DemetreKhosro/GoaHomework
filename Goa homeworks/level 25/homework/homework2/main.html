What is Crypto?

Crypto, (short for cryptocurrency) is digital money that only exists online and isnt issued by the government or any banks.

It operates by a decentralized networks of computers using a technology called blockchain

A blockchain is a distributed digital ledger that records all transactions across a network.

Everytime someone completes a transaction, its grouped with other transactions into a block, then that block is verified and added to a chain of previous transactions.

There are thousands of different cryptocurrencies, the most popular and expensive one is BitCoin.

There are other popular currencies like Ethereum, Dogecoin and etc.

Some people think Crypto is the future of finance and it will play a big role, while some people are completely against that, they are convinced it will be used in illegal activities.
