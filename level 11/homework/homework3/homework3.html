Section 1: What is GOA?

Goal-Oriented Academy offers you or your kid the best environment for learning programming. The professional mentors and group leaders will make sure you learn programming at the highest level

In groups there are group leaders and mini-leaders wich help you if you have any problems understanding or doing homework/classwork. You will also get to know other people and become friends with them or do group projects with them.


Section 2: What will you learn at GOA?

At GOA you will learn everything about programming. There are courses like programming and leadership, robotics, AI and algorithms and game development. Each of these courses have professional mentors that will teach you everything about their courses.

You will be able to make high-quality websites, games, robots or algorithms based on what path you choose.
